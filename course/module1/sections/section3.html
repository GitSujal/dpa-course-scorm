Data Quality and Governance

The Importance of Data Quality

Quality Impact

High-quality data is essential for making informed decisions. Poor data quality can lead to:

  • Incorrect business decisions
  • Lost revenue opportunities
  • Decreased customer satisfaction
  • Wasted resources and time

Key Dimensions of Data Quality

Data quality is measured across several critical dimensions that determine its usefulness and reliability.

Essential Quality Dimensions

  • Accuracy: Data correctly represents the real-world entity or event
  • Completeness: All required data is present and usable
  • Consistency: Data values are the same across all systems
  • Timeliness: Data is up-to-date and available when needed
  • Validity: Data follows defined business rules and formats

Data Governance Framework

Data governance ensures proper data management through:

  • Clear policies and procedures
  • Defined roles and responsibilities
  • Data security and privacy measures
  • Quality control processes
  • Regular audits and monitoring
